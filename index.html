Scenario 1

There are two envelopes. In the envelope handed to you there is definitely $X. In the other envelope, there is either $2X or $0. Whether or not the other envelope contains $2X or $0 on this particular occasion has already been decided, say by toss of fair coin (i.e. it's random), and so the other envelope already contains either $2X or $0, each outcome being 50% likely.

Do you KEEP or SWAP?
